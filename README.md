## Problem Statement

Many healthcare centers, especially in rural and semi-urban areas, lack round-the-clock access to radiologists, leading to delays in interpreting medical scans, poor patient understanding of results, weak triage systems, late diagnosis, and overcrowding of higher-level hospitals.

---

## Overview

ScanWise Medical Triage is a **cloud-based AI healthcare support system** that analyzes medical scans and lab reports, generates easy-to-understand summaries, and classifies cases into urgency levels such as Emergency, Priority, or Routine.

The system supports:
- Uploading medical scans and reports  
- AI-based scan and report analysis  
- Patient-friendly explanation of findings  
- Automated urgency classification  
- Guidance on next steps for patients and doctors  

The platform is software-only and designed for use in clinics, hospitals, and remote healthcare centers.

---

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** React.js + TypeScript  
- **Backend:** Node.js with Express  
- **AI Engine:** Gemini Vision + LLM  
- **Database:** Firebase Firestore  
- **Storage:** Firebase Cloud Storage  
- **Authentication:** Firebase Auth  
- **APIs:** Google Maps API, Gemini Vision API  

---

## Novelty Features

- **Triage Intelligence:** Converts AI-generated findings into Emergency, Priority, or Routine levels.  
- **Action-Oriented Output:** Provides next-step guidance instead of only raw reports.  
- **Patient-Friendly Language:** Explains findings in simple, non-diagnostic terms.  
- **Multi-Input Support:** Works with X-rays, MRI, Ultrasound, skin images, and lab reports.
